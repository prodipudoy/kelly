Trauma is the response to a deeply distressing or disturbing event that overwhelms an individual’s ability to cope, causes feelings of helplessness, diminishes their sense of self and their ability to feel the full range of emotions and experiences. Sometimes this trauma is passed from one generation to the other and is known as inter-generational trauma. Inter-generational trauma can be passed through behavioural problems, violence, parenting practices or mental health issues.
